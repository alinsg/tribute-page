A creative monkey's tools

CreativeMonkeyz.com it's a site dedicated to art (digital and traditional art). The site contains video tutorials in romanian for many programs of the Adobe Systems and inspirational galleries - free graphical elements wich can be used in commercial projects. Their content is free! It's the site of the popular web series RObotzi.

A few things that these awesome people do:
  • RObotzi is one of the most known web series in Romania. It's about the adventures of MO and F.O.C.A
  • LaCafea – it's airing everyday, monday to friday, on their Twitch.tv channel, where they debate all sorts of themes.
  • Piramida – a series presented by El Iobag Dromadero where the presenter is commenting some viral clips.
  • Daily livestream on Twitch.tv - with Codin and "Ninja Ramo" (Ramona), the founders of the Creative Monkeyz, and their awesome community that they created.
